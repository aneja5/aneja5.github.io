About Me

Welcome to my personal website! I’m Sahilpreet Aneja, a driven engineer passionate about combining mechanical and computer science skills to create innovative solutions. My academic journey began with a Bachelor’s degree in Mechatronics Engineering from Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology (TIET), where I honed my expertise in robotics, control systems, and embedded systems. Now, I’m expanding my knowledge further as I pursue a Master of Science in Computer Science at Northeastern University, with an expected graduation in May 2026.

During my academic career, I’ve had the opportunity to work on several impactful projects, including developing a Ride-Sharing Driver Validation System using Java and designing a Self-Posture Correcting Chair utilizing CAD and MATLAB simulations. My hands-on experience spans from creating software systems to designing mechanical components, giving me a unique perspective on how to integrate hardware and software seamlessly.

Professionally, I’ve gained valuable experience as an Assistant Manager at Hero MotoCorp, where I led a team in digitizing maintenance systems and integrating IoT sensors to reduce downtime by 25%. My ability to analyze workflows and implement process improvements also helped in reducing energy consumption significantly. I’ve been fortunate to apply my skills to real-world problems and contribute to large-scale operations in the industry.

I’m proficient in several programming languages, including Java, Python, C++, and SQL, and I regularly use tools such as MATLAB, SOLIDWORKS, Ansys, and various integrated development environments like Eclipse and IntelliJ IDEA. I am passionate about working on cutting-edge technologies that push the boundaries of what’s possible, especially in robotics and automation.

Beyond my technical pursuits, I am also an avid traveler who loves exploring new places and cultures. Traveling has broadened my worldview, inspired my creativity, and given me a fresh perspective on solving problems.

Feel free to explore more about my projects, read my blog, or get in touch. I'm excited to share my journey and look forward to connecting with you!
